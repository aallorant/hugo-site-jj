The **Friends of Jeanne Jozon** brings together descendants, scholars, curators, and enthusiasts around the memory and the work of a sculptor too long forgotten. The association serves as the steward of the artist's family and professional archives, and as the point of contact for any rediscovery initiative — exhibitions, publications, loans, academic research.

---

## Mission

The association pursues four objectives:

- **Make known** Jeanne Jozon's œuvre, in France and to the American public, where she retains a circle of collectors.
- **Preserve** the family archives — correspondence, sketchbooks, photographs of works, contracts — and the works held within the family and by collectors.
- **Document** the historical and artistic context: the Republican bourgeoisie of the late 19th century, the Académie Julian (a private Paris academy that, exceptionally for the period, admitted women), the Paris salons, and the production for the manufactures of Sèvres and Mehun-sur-Yèvre.
- **Promote** a singular voice of French Art Nouveau — that of an independent woman artist who explicitly chose celibacy to safeguard her creative freedom.

---

## Activities

Several initiatives run in parallel:

- **Inventory and documentation** of the dispersed œuvre — sculptures, jewelry, drawings, pastels, ceramics.
- **Contacts with museums** that may host an exhibition: Dinan, Issoudun, Pont-Aven, the Musée du Romantisme, Bourges.
- **Publication project** for the family and professional correspondence of Jeanne Jozon, and a definitive biography.
- **Documentary project** submitted to France Télévision.
- **Lectures and study days** on the artist and her milieu.

News and events are announced on the [News](/en/news/) page.

## Becoming a member

Membership is open to anyone interested in Jeanne Jozon's œuvre — family, scholarly, or simply curious. The modest annual subscription helps fund the inventory work, the digitization of archives, and the organization of lectures.

To join or make a donation, [contact us](/en/contact/) — an association bank account is open with Crédit Mutuel.

## Partnership inquiries

For any institutional partnership proposal — exhibition, loan, archival access for research, editorial or audiovisual project — please get in touch via the [Contact](/en/contact/) page.

We consider every serious request and prioritize partnerships with:

- **museums and cultural institutions** wishing to programme a Jozon exhibition;
- **researchers and academics** working on Art Nouveau, the Académie Julian, or women artists of the *Belle Époque*;
- **publishers and producers** interested in a publication or documentary;
- **collectors** holding works by the artist who would like them referenced in the inventory.
