# Salons & Exhibitions

## Forty-five years of artistic career

From **1895** to **1939**, Jeanne Jozon exhibited regularly in the most prestigious Paris salons and took part in international shows from Rouen to New York by way of Chicago.

A note on names: throughout this chronology, salon and society names — *Salon des Artistes Français*, *Exposition des Femmes Artistes*, *La Cimaise*, *La Nationale*, *Société Nationale des Beaux-Arts*, *Union Centrale des Arts Décoratifs* — are kept in French, since these are proper names of the historical institutions. Work titles are likewise kept in French; an English gloss is given on first appearance.
